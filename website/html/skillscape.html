Skillscape

Work
Motion
Year
2025
Instructor
David Cocklin

Skillscape is a self-promotional video that introduces my creative abilities. As a senior approaching graduation, I explored ways to present myself and chose to express this metaphorically through motion. In the video, a sphere represents myself, drifting through a dream-like sequence. Within this dream, it moves across and engages with various elements that symbolize my skills. Through this structure, the work presents my capabilities in a narrative and visual way.
