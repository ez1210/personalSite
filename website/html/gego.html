Gego Exhibition Rebranding

This project involved rebranding the exhibition Gego: Measuring Infinity, which was held at the Guggenheim Museum. The overall visual identity was developed from the woven patterns found in selected works by Gego. These weaving served as a key reference, informing the graphic language and unifying the design elements across the project.
