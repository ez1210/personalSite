vitra Catalog

In this project, I designed a catalog for the furniture company Vitra. Criteria for furniture selection was design principle. For example, Prismatic Table fits with the design principle ‘pattern’. In this rule, I chose 7 different furnitures and made different layouts that also fits for their own design principle.
