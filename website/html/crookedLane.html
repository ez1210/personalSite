Crooked Lane

Visual Identity, Branding, Editorial 2025 Instructor: Courtney Gooch

The Chocolate Factory Theater is a nonprofit organization in New York City that supports experimental performance across theater, dance, and multimedia art. It emphasizes artistic risk and process, providing a platform for diverse contemporary practices. Inspired by its name, I developed a visual identity based on the material quality of chocolate. I focused on the cracks formed when chocolate breaks, using them as a key design element to express a sense of expansion and freedom. This reflects the organization’s openness to experimentation and evolving forms. The main typography, Refinery 25 Semi Bold, was chosen to evoke the raw, industrial character of its repurposed building space.
