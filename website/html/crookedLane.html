Crooked Lane

Visual Identity, Branding, Editorial 2025 Instructor: Courtney Gooch

Crooked Lane Books is an independent publisher specializing in mystery, thriller, and suspense fiction. I developed the branding by focusing on its name and genre. Reflecting its focus on horror and mystery, I designed the logo with a crooked and irregular form rather than a straight path. The logo was then used as a key design element to create a cohesive visual identity.
